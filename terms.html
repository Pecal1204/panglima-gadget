Service Policies

Terms & Conditions

These policies cover the four things referenced on our homepage: our Google rating, same-day service, free diagnostics, and repair warranty.

Note: the details below are general guidance. Exact timelines, fees, and warranty coverage can vary by device and fault — always confirmed with you on WhatsApp before any work begins.

Google Rating

Our 5.0 rating reflects genuine reviews left by customers on our Google Business Profile. We don't filter, remove, or incentivize reviews — what you see is what customers have told us directly.

Ratings can change over time as new reviews come in. You can read all of our reviews on our reviews page.

Same-Day Repairs

Most common repairs — screen replacement, battery replacement, and charging port repair — are typically completed the same day you bring your device in, subject to parts being in stock.

  • More involved repairs (motherboard-level faults, extensive water damage, data recovery) may take longer.
  • If a part needs to be ordered in, we'll let you know an updated timeline before starting work.
  • Same-day service is offered on a best-effort basis and isn't guaranteed for every device or fault.

Free Diagnostics

We inspect your device and identify the fault at no charge. You'll always get a clear explanation of the issue and a firm price quote before any repair work begins — no surprise charges.

If you choose not to proceed with the repair after diagnosis, you're free to walk away with no diagnostic fee. Advanced diagnostics (such as in-depth data recovery assessment) may be a separate service — we'll always tell you upfront if that applies.

Service Warranty

Repairs carried out by Panglima Gadget are backed by a service warranty covering defects in the parts we install and the workmanship of the repair itself.

  • The warranty applies to the specific issue that was repaired — it doesn't cover new physical damage, liquid damage, or faults unrelated to the original repair.
  • Warranty coverage may be voided if the device is subsequently opened or repaired elsewhere.
  • Ask us for the exact warranty duration for your specific repair — we'll confirm it in writing (WhatsApp) at the time of service.
